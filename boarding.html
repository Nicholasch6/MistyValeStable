Boarding

We offer field board and stall board! Whatever level of service you require for your horse, we make sure they remain healthy, safe, and well cared for.

Field board

Basic field board includes hay when pasture is not sufficient, and can include several options depending on your horses needs. Full service field board includes

  • Basic Field Board: $190 per Month
  • Full service field board: $240 per month

Stall Board

Stall Board includes all services included in full service field board. Stalled horses will be turned out for the most time possible depending on the weather (overnight during the hotter months, during the day during colder weather).

Additional boarding services can be provided as required, though may come at additional cost depending on the nature of the service. If your horse needs additional services not listed here, please contact us directly for more information.
