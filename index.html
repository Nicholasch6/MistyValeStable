Misty Vale Stables is a a small family farm located west of Hagerstown Maryland. Owner and operator Mary Schneider has over 35 years of experience in training, instructing, and equine management. Misty Vale is a family oriented stable, which is open to riders of all disciplines and levels of experience. We have well trained horses for lease and our students have had great success at shows.

Boarding

We offer field board and stall board! Whatever level of service you require for your horse, we make sure they remain healthy, safe, and well cared for.

  • Basic Field Board starts at $190 per Month
  • Stall Board starts at $350 per Month

Basic field board includes hay when pasture is not sufficient, and can include several options depending on your horses needs.

Stall Board includes grain feed. Stalled horses will be turned out for the most time possible depending on the weather (overnight during the hotter months, during the day in colder weather).

The prices listed here are the base price for boarding. Additional boarding services can be provided depending on your horses needs, though they may come at additional cost depending on the nature of the service and supplies required. Please call us to discuss your horses boarding arrangments to determine the total charges for boarding.
