Apple had puppies!


Our wonderful farm Golden retriever has had puppies of her own! Apple gave birth to a healthy litter of 8 puppies in Late June. There are four boys and four girls. They are all purebred Golden Retrievers with AKC papers.

The puppies are for sale for $1,200 each. We love Apple and all her puppies, and we want to make sure they all go to good homes, so we will be a bit selective about who we sell to.
